關於我

我承認我是個怪人 喜歡研究人們的價值觀、思維、以及行動 不對任何事隨意做出判斷 自己對於各種上來說 耐心誇張到了固執無可救藥的地步 也因為從小的教育環境 使得自己思想過於負面 更在高職的時候 聽聞了各式不同的故事 家暴、離婚、輟學、雙親早逝等等 每個人都有自己的故事 造就了現在的我-----心靈傾聽者。

從6歲開始，我開始寫詩記下自己當下的想法或價值觀，或者周遭朋友遭遇的事情，所有的一切都被我記錄在紙筆上 到了現在，早已累積無數詩作，未來也考慮將其分享在自己的IG或者BLOG上 寫詩對我來說也是一種放鬆心情的方法，把自己心裡的想法，不加掩飾得揮灑在一行行紙筆上， 會有一種一身輕的感覺，也就是認為自己是新詩作者的原因。

I admit that I'm a freak and I like to study people's values, thinking and actions I don't judge anything at will. For all kinds of things, my patience exaggerates to the point of being stubborn and hopeless. It is also because of the education environment since childhood, which makes me think too much in a negative way. In higher vocational education, I heard different stories Family violence, divorce, dropping out of school, early death of both parents and so on, everyone has their own story, which has created me now - the soul listener.

From the age of six, I began to write poems to record my current thoughts or values, or what happened to my friends around me, all of which were recorded on paper and pen Up to now, countless poems have been accumulated, and it is also considered to be shared on its own Ig or blog in the future Writing poetry is also a way for me to relax. I can spread my thoughts in my heart on a line of paper and pen without any concealment, There will be a feeling of lightness, that is to say, the reason why I think I am a new poet.
